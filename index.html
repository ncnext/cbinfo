Who is the Real Cherie Berry?

She wants you to think she's a friendly "elevator lady."

But who is she, really?

As North Carolina's Labor Commissioner, she's supposed to be the state's chief advocate for workers, but here's what Cherie Berry really cares about...

The Elevator Lady Wants to Keep Your Wages in the Basement

  • Cherie Berry doesn't believe North Carolina should even HAVE a minimum wage!

The Elevator Lady Gives Workers the Shaft

Cherie Berry has been investigated repeatedly. Here's what we've learned:

  • She rarely forces payments from companies that cheat workers
  • her Wage and Hour program is biased against employees in favor of employers
  • an audit of Berry's NCDOL revealed misclassification and downplaying of the seriousness of safety violations in North Carolina workplaces
  • Cherie Berry doesn’t see her mission as protecting workers; she sees it as protecting business, even when workers are getting shortchanged. Time and again, her department has fallen short on investigations of unpaid wages...
  • Under Berry, NCDOL has been undercounting the number of workers who die on the job in North Carolina every year

The Elevator Lady in Her Own Words

  • Cherie Berry cherishes “small little victories” over federal labor officials trying to protect workers
  • Cherie Berry wants the federal government to get off her back
  • What Cherie Berry really cares about is "scaling back many of the burdensome regulations that strangle our business... I fight a constant battle with the Obama administration's Labor Department"
