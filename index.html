Who is the Real Cherie Berry?

She wants you to think she's a friendly "elevator lady."

But who is she, really?

Cherie Berry doesn't believe North Carolina should even have a minimum wage.

She's supposed to be the state's chief advocate for workers, but here's what she really cares about: "scaling back many of the burdensome regulations that strangle our business... I fight a constant battle with the Obama administration's Labor Department"

The Elevator Lady Gives Workers the Shaft

She's been investigated repeatedly. Here's what we've learned:

  • She rarely forces payments from companies that cheat workers
  • her Wage and Hour program is biased against employees in favor of employers
  • an audit of Berry's NCDOL revealed misclassification and downplaying of the seriousness of safety violations in workplaces
  • Cherie Berry doesn’t see her mission as protecting workers; she sees it as protecting business, even when workers are getting shortchanged. Time and again, her department has fallen short on investigations of unpaid wages...
  • Under Berry, NCDOL has been undercounting the number of workers who die on the job in North Carolina every year

The Elevator Lady in Her Own Words

  • She cherishes “small little victories” over federal labor officials because she wants the federal government to get off her back
